Mercury

Mercury, the innermost planet of the solar system, is the most elusive planet to observe. Its closeness to the Sun makes it visible only during twilight.

Mercury is the closest planet to the Sun, taking sunlight only 3.2 minutes to reach it. Its distance is approximately 57.91 million kilometers from the Sun.
